The Apex Simulator

Where we
actually teach.

The Redbird FMX AATD isn't a supplement — it's the primary classroom. Pause. Repeat. Debrief. Run it again. Once it's automatic in the sim, we take it to the aircraft.

  • Up to 50 hours toward commercial certificate
  • Full-motion Redbird FMX with G1000 avionics suite
  • ILS, RNAV, VOR approaches in any simulated weather
