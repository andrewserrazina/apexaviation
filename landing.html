Ground School Series

Three series.
One standard.

Attend any module from any series — modules are self-contained and can be taken in any order. Complete all modules in a series to earn your knowledge test endorsement.

Private Pilot Series
Private Pilot Ground School
Everything in the PAR — from aerodynamics and airspace to weather and cross-country planning. Built for student pilots starting from zero.
  • Aerodynamics & aircraft systems
  • Weather theory & decision-making
  • Airspace & regulations
  • VFR navigation & charts
  • Emergency procedures & ADM
  • PAR knowledge test endorsement
$25 / session
Instrument Series
Instrument Rating Ground School
IFR regulations, navigation systems, approach procedures, and weather for instrument flight. Built for VFR pilots adding their instrument rating.
  • IFR regulations & flight planning
  • Navigation systems & avionics
  • Instrument approach procedures
  • Holding patterns & en route ops
  • IFR weather & decision-making
  • IRA knowledge test endorsement
$25 / session
Commercial Series
Commercial Pilot Ground School
Advanced aerodynamics, commercial regulations, and complex systems for the CAX. Built for instrument-rated pilots pursuing their commercial certificate.
  • Advanced aerodynamics & performance
  • Commercial regulations (Parts 61, 119)
  • Complex aircraft systems
  • Weight & balance & planning
  • Commercial maneuvers theory
  • CAX knowledge test endorsement
$25 / session
