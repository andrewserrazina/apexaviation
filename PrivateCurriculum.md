# APEX ADVANTAGE PRIVATE PILOT MASTER CURRICULUM
Version 1.0
July 2026

---

# PURPOSE

This document serves as the master curriculum blueprint for the Apex Advantage Private Pilot Ground School.

All lessons, slide decks, portal content, quizzes, checkride preparation materials, instructor guides, and downloadable resources must align with this curriculum.

This curriculum is designed to:

- Meet and exceed FAA Airman Certification Standards (ACS)
- Prepare students for the FAA Knowledge Test
- Prepare students for the Private Pilot Oral Exam
- Develop real-world aeronautical decision-making skills
- Build confident and competent pilots

The objective is not simply to pass a test.

The objective is to create safe, knowledgeable, and capable aviators.

# COURSE OUTCOMES

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:

- Demonstrate knowledge required by FAA ACS standards
- Pass the FAA Private Pilot Knowledge Test
- Successfully complete a Private Pilot Oral Exam
- Apply aeronautical decision making principles
- Operate safely within the National Airspace System
- Plan and execute VFR cross-country flights
- Understand aircraft systems and limitations
- Manage risk effectively
