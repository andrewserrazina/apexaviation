How We Started

A School Born From a Passion for Flight

Apex Aviation was founded in 2004 by Captain James Harrington, a former regional airline captain who wanted to create the flight school he wished had existed when he was learning to fly — one where students are treated as the next generation of professional aviators, not just paying customers.

What started as a two-aircraft operation has grown into one of the region's most respected flight training academies, with a fleet of 15+ aircraft, a full-motion simulator, and a team of instructors who collectively have logged over 80,000 flight hours across every category of aviation.

Our mission has never changed: produce pilots who are not just certificate holders, but truly safe and capable aviators who are ready for whatever comes next — whether that's weekend cross-countries or an airline cockpit.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

Everything we do is guided by three core principles that have defined Apex Aviation since day one.

Safety First

Every decision we make — from aircraft maintenance to training syllabus design — starts with safety. We train pilots to exercise sound judgment, not just follow checklists. Our culture of safety is why our graduates have an exceptional safety record.

Expert Instruction

We hire only highly experienced instructors with real-world aviation backgrounds — not newly certificated CFIs looking for a quick hour-building job. Every instructor at Apex has at least 1,500 hours and brings genuine expertise to every lesson.

Your Goals, Our Mission

Every student is different. Whether you want to fly recreationally on weekends or pursue a career at a major airline, we tailor your training path, schedule, and pace to your personal goals. Your success is our success.
