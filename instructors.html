Andrew Serrazina

Founder & Chief Flight Instructor

CFI CFII AGI Comm. ASEL Comm. AMEL Inst. Airplane

Andrew Serrazina is the Founder and Chief Flight Instructor of Apex Aviation, where he is committed to redefining flight training through modern instruction, advanced technology, and a student-first approach. With a passion for aviation education and a dedication to developing safe, proficient pilots, Andrew has helped aspiring and experienced aviators build the knowledge, confidence, and decision-making skills required for success in the cockpit.

Andrew holds Commercial Pilot certificates with Airplane Single-Engine Land and Multi-Engine Land privileges, along with an Instrument Rating and Flight Instructor Certificate. He has extensive experience teaching in both technically advanced aircraft and conventional flight decks, enabling him to tailor instruction to pilots at every stage of their training.

Beyond flight instruction, Andrew is a recognized aviation educator whose online content has reached more than 2.5 million viewers across multiple platforms. His practical, scenario-based teaching style has earned a reputation for making complex aviation concepts accessible, engaging, and directly applicable to real-world flying.

Andrew is currently pursuing a Master of Science in Aeronautics with an emphasis on aviation safety, leadership, and human factors. His vision for Apex Aviation is to create a training environment that combines personalized instruction, immersive simulator technology, and a culture of professionalism to prepare pilots not only to pass checkrides, but to become confident, capable aviators throughout their flying careers.
