Flight Simulator Training for New Braunfels Pilots

New Braunfels sits midway between Austin and San Antonio — 45 minutes from the Apex facility and home to New Braunfels Municipal Airport (KBAZ). Apex Advantage ground school launches September 2026; the Austin AATD simulator facility opens January 2027.

Serving Pilots from New Braunfels and the Surrounding Area

New Braunfels has seen extraordinary growth over the last decade, drawing professionals and families from both Austin and San Antonio who now call the Comal County corridor home. Many arrive as existing pilots looking to maintain or regain currency, or as motivated students who've spent years wanting to learn to fly but never found the right environment. New Braunfels Municipal Airport (KBAZ) is a well-equipped general aviation field, but access to advanced scenario-based simulator training has historically required a substantial drive in either direction on I-35. Apex Aviation brings professional AATD instruction within reach — with a curriculum designed around real flying decisions, not just checkride minimums.

What New Braunfels Pilots Train For at Apex

Private Pilot Training

Build airmanship fundamentals in the simulator before applying them in the FlightDesign CTSW at KHYI.

Why Sim Training Makes Sense for New Braunfels Pilots

Flying out of New Braunfels puts you squarely between two major Class C airspace environments — Austin-Bergstrom (KAUS) and San Antonio International (KSAT) — and right in the path of weather systems that funnel up from the Gulf through the Hill Country. The combination of complex ATC, fast-moving convective weather, and the visual illusions that come with Hill Country terrain makes New Braunfels one of the best-motivated communities for serious scenario training. Simulator sessions at Apex let you rehearse ATC transitions, practice holds in a KSAT terminal environment, and work through deteriorating VMC scenarios repeatedly — without weather cancellations, fuel burn, or having to actually enter a busy Class C to practice.
