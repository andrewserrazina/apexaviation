Why Commercial

The commercial certificate is the gateway to flying for compensation or hire. Whether you're targeting a CFI certificate, charter operations, or the airlines, commercial is the foundation every professional pilot builds on.

At Apex, we use maximum AATD credit under §61.129 — up to 50 hours — to keep your total cost realistic without compromising the quality of your airmanship. The sim builds precision. The aircraft proves it.
