Private Pilot Series
Private Pilot

Private Pilot Ground School

Covers everything in the PAR: aerodynamics, weather, airspace, regulations, navigation, and cross-country planning. Built for student pilots starting from zero.

  • Aerodynamics & aircraft systems
  • Weather theory & decision-making
  • Airspace & regulations
  • VFR navigation & charts
  • Emergency procedures & ADM
  • PAR knowledge test endorsement

Instrument Series
Instrument Rating

Instrument Rating Ground School

Covers IFR regulations, navigation systems, approach procedures, en route operations, and weather for instrument flight. Built for VFR pilots adding their instrument rating.

  • IFR regulations & flight planning
  • Navigation systems & avionics
  • Instrument approach procedures
  • Holding patterns & en route ops
  • IFR weather & decision-making
  • IRA knowledge test endorsement

Commercial Series
Commercial Pilot

Commercial Pilot Ground School

Covers advanced aerodynamics, commercial regulations, complex systems, and the performance knowledge required for the CAX. Built for instrument-rated pilots pursuing their commercial certificate.

  • Advanced aerodynamics & performance
  • Commercial regulations (Parts 61, 119, 135)
  • Complex & high-performance aircraft systems
  • Weight & balance & cross-country planning
  • Commercial maneuvers theory
  • CAX knowledge test endorsement
