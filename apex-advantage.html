Class Format

What to Expect
Each Week

Every Apex Advantage session follows a consistent, structured format — covering core concepts, real-world application, checkride preparation, and open Q&A. Active participation is encouraged in every block.

Part 01

Knowledge Session

Core aviation concepts taught live by instructors. This is classroom instruction — not lecture-to-video. Students ask questions as concepts are introduced and the instructor adjusts based on the room.

Part 02

Scenario Workshop

Students apply the session's concepts to realistic aviation situations and decision-making exercises. This is where theory becomes judgment — and where the gaps in rote memorization become visible.

Part 03

Checkride Corner

The instructor walks through how FAA examiners typically evaluate and discuss the session topic during oral and practical tests — so students know exactly what they'll face and how to respond.

Part 04

Open Q&A

Dedicated time for students to ask anything — from session content to training questions to real-world flying situations. Direct answers from instructors who have been there.
