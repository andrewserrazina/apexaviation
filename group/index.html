Who We Are

Modern aviation education, built beyond the minimum standard.

Apex Aviation Training Group LLC is the company behind Apex Advantage, a training platform for private, instrument, and commercial pilot candidates. The mission is straightforward: provide professional, checkride-focused aviation education that goes beyond minimum FAA standards and prepares students to become safer, more competent pilots.

Apex Advantage combines live instructor-led training, self-paced online learning, interactive scenario-based education, checkride preparation resources, and a growing aviation community — bridging the gap between traditional ground school and real-world pilot decision-making.

The company is led by a working Commercial Pilot and Certified Flight Instructor, and every product decision is made by someone who is directly in the training environment with students, not removed from it.

What We Do

Three ways we deliver aviation education.

Apex Advantage is designed as a coordinated system, not a single product — live instruction, self-paced digital learning, and (starting January 2027) simulator-based training in one facility.

01

Live Instructor-Led Training

Ground school and checkride preparation delivered directly by an active Commercial Pilot and CFI, not a pre-recorded course with no instructor contact.

02

Self-Paced Digital Portal

A free member portal with a 256-question DPE-style Checkride Prep System, progress tracking, and scenario-based study tools available today.

03

Simulator-Based Training

An FAA-certified AATD simulator facility opening in Austin, Texas, extending the platform into hands-on, scenario-based flight training.
