Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Apex Aviation is a flight training company based in Austin, Texas. We operate at apexaviationtx.com and can be reached at info@apexaviationtx.com.

What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Submit the contact form (name, email, message)
  • Join the waitlist (name, email)
  • Book a session through Calendly (name, email, and any details you provide during scheduling)
  • Send us an email directly

We do not collect payment information directly — billing is handled through third-party processors.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule training sessions
  • Send updates about your waitlist status and program availability
  • Communicate about your training progress
  • Send occasional email updates about Apex Aviation (you can unsubscribe at any time)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Formspree — processes contact and waitlist form submissions. See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • Calendly — handles session scheduling. See Calendly's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces from Google's servers. See Google's privacy policy.

Cookies

The Apex Aviation website does not use tracking cookies or analytics. Third-party services (Calendly, Google Fonts) may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

Data retention

We retain your contact information for as long as we have an active relationship with you or as required for business purposes. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing info@apexaviationtx.com.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from any communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@apexaviationtx.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@apexaviationtx.com.
