About Apex Aviation

Built by Pilots,
For Pilots

Apex Aviation was founded with a single mission: to produce safe, skilled, and confident aviators. Our team of dedicated instructors brings decades of real-world flight experience — from bush flying to airline operations — into every lesson.

We believe flight training should be rigorous, rewarding, and accessible. Whether you're taking your first discovery flight or pursuing a commercial certificate, we tailor every syllabus to your goals and schedule.

  • Part 141 & Part 61 training pathways
  • Full-motion flight simulators on-site
  • Financing options available
  • Flexible scheduling — evenings & weekends
