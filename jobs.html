Ground Instruction · Virtual · Part-Time

Apex Advantage Ground Instructor

Starting September 2026

Apex Advantage ground school launches in September 2026 with live, virtual sessions covering Private Pilot, Instrument Rating, and Commercial Pilot knowledge. We're looking for an AGI or CFI with a genuine ability to explain aviation concepts clearly — someone who can hold a room (even a virtual one), answer the question behind the question, and make regulations feel like something worth understanding.

Sessions run Monday through Thursday on a set schedule. You'll teach one or more certificate tracks depending on your background and availability. All curriculum is provided — your job is to bring it to life.

What You'll Do
  • ✓Lead live virtual ground school sessions (PPL, IFR, and/or CPL)
  • ✓Answer student questions live and provide written exam endorsements
  • ✓Follow the Apex curriculum and deliver to the Apex standard
  • ✓Collaborate with Andrew on curriculum refinement and student progress
What We're Looking For
  • ✓FAA AGI or CFI certificate (required)
  • ✓Strong command of FAA ACS standards across certificate levels
  • ✓Comfortable leading live virtual instruction (camera-on, engaged)
  • ✓Clear communicator who can adapt to different learning styles
